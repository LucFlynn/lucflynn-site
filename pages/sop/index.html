Quick Wins

  • Call + form extensions: Add them to every ad. They raise CTR by 10–15%.
  • Ad scheduling: Only run ads when someone can answer calls. Don't pay for 2 a.m. leads.
  • Fast follow-up: Call back within 5 minutes. Response speed doubles close rates.
  • Automate alerts: Use GTM, Zapier, or rules to pause losing keywords or alert you when CPL jumps.
  • Keep testing: There's always one ad, page, or keyword that beats the rest. Find it.
