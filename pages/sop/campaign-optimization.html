Google Ads SOP — Campaign Optimization

Fix structure, align targeting and creative, and reallocate budget with confidence. Updated Nov 4, 2025

Objective

Diagnose and repair performance at the campaign/ad‑group layer; ensure ads and landing pages match intent; push budget to winners and stop waste.

1) Structural & Performance Audit

  • Review budgets, bidding strategy, locations, devices, audiences per campaign.
  • Metrics by campaign/ad group: Spend, Conversions, CPA/ROAS, CTR, CPC, Impression Share.
  • Identify losers (high spend / low conv) and winners (stable CPA, strong CVR).

2) Relevance: Ad → Page → Intent

  • Check each ad group’s RSA quality (aim for Good or Excellent).
  • Confirm landing page carries the same promise and has a clear CTA.
  • High bounce/low CVR groups: revise copy or route to a better page.

3) Action: Pause, Test, or Scale

Pause

Ad groups or campaigns missing targets after 2–4 weeks with adequate volume.

Test

Throttle budget, fix targeting/creative/page, set a review date.

Scale

Winners with stable CPA/ROAS → see Scaling SOP.

4) Targeting & Controls

  • Geo: exclude low‑performing areas; increase bids where CVR is strong.
  • Device: adjust bids for mobile/desktop based on CPA/CVR.
  • Schedule: ad scheduling to protect CPA; dayparting if needed.
  • Audiences: layer in‑market/remarketing where appropriate.

5) Creative Rotation

  • Maintain ≥1 high‑quality RSA per ad group; add variants regularly.
  • Test headlines/CTAs; include high‑intent keywords naturally.
  • Refresh fatigued ads; track changes and results.

6) Bids, Budgets & Pacing

  • Manual: adjust bids to target CPA, protect Impression Share on winners.
  • Automated: verify target (tCPA/tROAS) and ensure sufficient conversion volume.
  • Watch daily pacing; avoid early caps that starve top performers.

7) Tracking & Page QA

  • Confirm conversion tracking integrity; dedupe events; verify attribution windows.
  • Landing page: mobile speed, clarity, single primary CTA, form/call tracking working.

Deliverables

  • Campaign/ad‑group status board (Continue / Reallocate / Pause / Test).
  • Targeting adjustments diff (geo/device/audience/schedule).
  • Creative changes log + RSA quality improvements.
  • Budget/bid change log and pacing notes.
  • Landing page QA checklist results.
