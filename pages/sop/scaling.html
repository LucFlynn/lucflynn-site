Google Ads SOP — Scaling

Grow volume while protecting unit economics. Updated Nov 4, 2025

Objective

Scale high‑performing campaigns/ad groups in controlled increments, with guardrails to preserve CPA/ROAS.

1) Readiness Checklist

  • Meets/exceeds target CPA or ROAS for last 30 days.
  • Conversion tracking accurate; sufficient volume for stable learning.
  • Keyword & campaign SOPs completed; obvious leaks patched.
  • Budget not constrained by caps; pacing healthy.

2) Scaling Methods

Vertical

  • Increase budget in increments (≤20% per step).
  • Protect Impression Share on winners.
  • Allow 7–10 days between steps for learning.

Horizontal

  • Add adjacent keywords/match types in a test ad group.
  • Expand to new geos/devices/audiences with small budgets.
  • Consider channel extensions (PMax/YouTube/Display) separately.
